Description

Bleeding Heart Botanically known as Clerodendrum thomsonae from Verbenaceae family. Common names are glory-bower, bagflower, bleeding-heart vine, etc. It is a perennial evergreen flowering vine with showy white flowers and carolla is bright red. The white sepals are long lasting & it requires trelling or chain like fencing or arbors for growing over it. It can also grow in pots/containers.
Plants have very showy flowers, which is an attractive bushy, tropical looking twining vine. Bleeding Heart Vine blooms profusely with rich crimson corollas peeking from white, balloon-like calyxes. The flowers are seen as clusters of red and white.They are good climbing plant.
